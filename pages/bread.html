Bread

Description

Basic food for basic needs; quite grainy, in fact

Ingredients

  • 1 cup warm water
  • 3 tbsp sugar
  • 1,5 tsp salt
  • 3 cups flour
  • 2,25 tsp active dry yeast

Steps

  1. Mix water, sugar, salt, oil, flour and yeast.
  2. Put it into a baking machine.
  3. Let the machine do the rest.
  4. Eat bread.
