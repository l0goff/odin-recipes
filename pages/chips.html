Chips

Description

Clogged arteries never tasted better!

Ingredients

  • potatoes
  • more potatoes
  • yes, even more potatoes, trust me
  • oil
  • salt
  • vinegar
  • curry sauce

Steps

  1. Wash and peel the potatos. All of them. Cut them into little bars.
  2. Heat the oil and fry the potatoes until they are golden.
  3. Take the potatoes out. Sprinkle them with salt.
  4. Use vinegar and/or curry sauce to taste.
  5. Bon appetit!
  6. Relish that you got so many potatoes. You can eat till you burst.
