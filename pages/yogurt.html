Yogurt

Description

Wobbly fermented milk

Ingredients

  • 1l milk
  • 1 tbsp white sugar
  • 2 tbsp yogurt with live cultures (for a starter)

Steps

  1. Heat milk to almost boiling point (85°C).
  2. Let it cool to approx. 40°C. At the same time, have the "old" yogurt warm to room temperature.
  3. Add the starter to the milk, pour the mixtures one or several container(s).
  4. Keep the yogurt warm (~40°C) and have it incubate for several hours.
  5. Eat yogurt.
